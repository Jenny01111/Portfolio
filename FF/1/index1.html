SPINNING HOUSE

n Bosnia, a 72-year-old man named Vojin Kusic built a revolving home for his wife, Ljubica. Because she wanted their bedroom and living room to be open to the sun and free to change the view from the window. Vojin's home rotates on a 7-meter axis. The slowest speed is 24 hours to complete a lap. The rotation can be completed in up to 22 seconds. Vojin Kusic borrowed an electric motor and wheels from an old military transport and built a revolving home with his two hands.
