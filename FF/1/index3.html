THE FLUTE

So far I have two flutes before and after, flute is accompany me the longest time of musical instrument, witness my growth, like my shadow, like my organ. My relationship with an object is not just that I own it when I buy it. My relationship with the flute is my relationship with myself. The way I relate to the flute reflects the way I relate to other individuals and to society. In this project, the flute is no longer an ordinary still life, I try to use my flute as a contact point, to express their emotional debt to their families and some reconciliation with their hearts. I try to explore in depth the ways in which memories and emotions of private objects relate to those of their owners, to themselves, to their surroundings, and to their social relationships, through the close connection between private objects and their owners.

Meaning: short-term relief from various human stressors, making it possible for them to love life in a better state, while making rational use of the happiness and emotions of their customers.
