THE COLOUR OF THE RAIN

Rainy Day is the most common, the rain single single single out, it is crystal clear, colorless. But to connect rain with everything in the world, to use rain as a medium, to see what the world looks like, I will use physical collage to express my perception of the color of rain, trying to explore the world from a new perspective, to perceive the connection between rain and the world and rain and the self.
